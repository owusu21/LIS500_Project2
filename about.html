Annie

Annie is currently living in Minneapolis, MN working a special education teacher. She is a graduate student studying library and information studies. In her free time she enjoys reading, playing video games, and crocheting. Annie is taking this course becuase she has a love for technology having started undergrad as a computer science major. She then changed to an elementary education major to connect more with people. Now she wants to combine the two in her graduate studies and hopes this course will help to provide a human perspective to technology.

RaeAnna

RaeAnna is currently a sophomore at the University of Wisconsin-Madison studying Legal Studies and Information Science. Originally from Ellsworth, Wisconsin, she enjoys music, excercising, and dance! She is interested in exploring the intersection between tech and law. She took this class because she is interested in exploring how disparities are present in technology and can affect the way we use technology while gaining skills with different computing projects. As a new student in the iSchool, she has felt the pressures of being intimated in classes made up of mostly men, which has made her curious about the disparities in the field. She believes this class perfectly blends her sociological academic studies with her technical ones.
